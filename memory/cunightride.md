# CU Night Ride — Navigation Memory

## Service Hours
- **Weekdays**: 7:00 PM – 12:00 AM (midnight)
- **Weekends**: 7:00 PM – 1:00 AM
- If the app shows "No trips available" or similar outside these hours, return success: false with an appropriate error message.

## UI Notes
- Home screen has a "Where to?" destination field and a pre-filled pickup field
- The pickup field is usually pre-filled with a campus/saved location — do NOT clear it with CTRL+A+DEL (doesn't work reliably). Instead, tap the field, look for an **X (clear) button** on the right side and tap it, then type the new address.
- After entering the dropoff, the app may show a "Confirm Pickup Location" screen — scroll down and tap the confirm button at the bottom.
- Ride options may have a "Free" filter pre-selected — if "No trips found" appears, look for filter buttons and try removing/changing them.
